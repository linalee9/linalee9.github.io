Let $G = (V, E)$ be a graph, i.e., $V$ is a set of nodes and $E$ is a set of edges connecting two nodes of $V$. $E\subset V\times V$ is defined as follows:
For $u,v\in V$,

$$
(u,v) \in E,\quad\text{if $u$ and $v$ are connected with an edge}
$$
